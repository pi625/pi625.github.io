**The Nullstellensatz** is my entry for the [Summer of Math Exposition 5](https://some.3b1b.co/).
Alon's combinatorial nullstellenatz is a useful tool in olympiad mathematics, where I first got introduced to it, and it is very versatile, being a helpful tool for many kinds of problems. However, the concept behind it is not that tricky. Thus, I'm going to build the concepts behind the nullstellensatz in my entry as intuitively as possible, with a lot of interactive elements.
